The purpose of this loan is to finance the purchase of Commercial Real Estate property. Examples: Apartment Building, Office Building, Hotel

CRE Loan: Re-Finance - Rate & Term

The purpose of this loan is to refinance a currently existing Commercial Real Estate Mortgage for a Commercial Property already owned by entity.

The purpose of this loan is to refinance a currently existing Commercial Mortgage with a new Commercial Real Estate Mortgage and receive funds.
